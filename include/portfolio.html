Proposal

As a part of Technical Communication, I was tasked with making a proposal of some sort for change within the college. My group decided on discussing the issue with textbooks and their outlandish pricing. We proposed moving away from paid textbooks and moving to free alternatives.

During our initial research to decide if it was worth our time to even propose this change, we discovered monetary benefit for the colleges and professors who choose to use free alternatives. Hopefully, our research during our Recomendation Report would support us in getting this project approved.

Recommendation Report

During this project we interviewed students and professors to determine the place of textbooks in the modern college setting. We ended up affirming our initial thoughts during the research conducted during our proposal project: Free resources are more beneficial to students than paid textbooks.

We ran into many obstacles. For most of us, this was our first college group project. We had previously worked in groups during high school, but college group work is totally different. Unlike in high school, we all have jobs and more serious work to get done, both in and out of class. We ended up scheduling around any obstacles and got our work taken care of. This project was quite the learning experience!
